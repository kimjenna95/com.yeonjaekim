American Party Animals

Goodbye campaign buttons, hello NFTs. American Party Animals (APA) are NFTs to influence Congress and create political change.

Project

American Party Animals is a quick project I took on for a client. The objective was to rebrand their landing page to match with their NFT artworks.

Before

The previous design (screenshot provided below) lacked a sense of branding that was consistent with the client's NFT artworks. It was a long page with redundant information and no clarity in what they were offering.

Solution

1. Communicated value proposition: Users should immediately be able to figure out what the page is about, and what product they offer.

2. Established a strong visual hierarchy: Considered size, placement, color, and position to make the design visually interesting and establish a hierarchy.

3. Simplified: Simplified the navigation to avoid and get rid of unnecessary navigation links.

4. Optimized conversion journey: Incorporated "click triggers" in appropriate areas of the page to motivate users to connect their wallet and purchase a NFT.
