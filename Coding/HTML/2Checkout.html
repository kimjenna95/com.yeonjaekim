2Checkout

Rebranding a platform that simplifies the back-end complexities and allowing businesses to quickly expand internationally and optimize recurring revenue streams across channels.

About

Redesigning a homepage for 2Checkout focusing on visual impacts (colors, styling, and typography), user experience and conversion.

Before

The previous design (screenshot provided below) was overly complicated with too many CTAs and content.

After

1. Allows the user to land on the page and read quick supporting info about the product/platform
2. Kept simple by providing only one main CTA button to prompt the user to click on it immediately
3. Hero illustration portrays a general understanding of what the platform offers in one view while creating a sense of branding
4. Changing the tone of voice of the microcopy and content to sound friendly and promising
5. Placing CTAs at the bottom of the page to increase conversion rate

ADA Compliant

As important as it is to think of aesthetics, we need to create accessible websites for all users. When designing, I tried to be considerate in the web accessibility standards by considering the following:

1. Enable keyboard navigation
2. Prioritizing text clarity
3. Not replying exclusively on color
