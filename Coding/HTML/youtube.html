Youtube- Case Study

Creating a video-centered portal that provides the convenience of searching, watching the video, and performing additional activities in order to perform the mediating role of communication between the YouTubers and the viewers.

About

Youtube is unanimously the most popular video platform in our society. Today, more people are suffering from a “burnout syndrome,” becoming lethargic due to exhaustion of mental and physical energy from their daily stress. We have become an ‘emotional consumer’, gaining surrogate satisfaction and learning through watching the experience of others.

Key Questions &
Guiding Process

What is the value YouTube wants to give to users?

Why do users use Youtube?

How can I provide a better way of what users want to get?

As a result of the research, it was found that users use YouTube to gain information or to be satisfied with the contents(experience) provided by YouTubers. As such, YouTube’s value lies in the ‘content’ itself. By devising a way that allows users to more conveniently access and acts on the content they are interested in, I intended to promote the convenience of the application and expand the user’s experience.

Goal

The goal of the project was to create a video-centered portal that provides the convenience of searching, watching the video, and performing additional activities in order to perform the mediating role of communication between the YouTubers and the viewers.

Field Research

Task-oriented observation and in-depth interview

Establish a research plan

Major issues are selected based on the results of desk research.

Fill out a questionnaire based on selected issues.

Select research targets

Select user targets based on age, YouTube app proficiency, and frequency of use.

Select in-depth interviewees through online surveys.

Conduct research

Observation surveys and in-depth interviews were conducted with the selected 6 people.

Draw insights

UX direction is established based on key findings derived through research result analysis.

What I Learned

I learned how important to be thinking of many different users other than one specific person. Every user has an individual problem and needs. It is important for the UX designer to keep account of this and make sure that all users have their needs met.
