"Life is Sweet"

Happiness is largely a matter of being thankful for the small things around us, not being greedy or desiring for more.
Life should be sweet. Life should be happy. And life should be grateful for itself.

The collection “Life is Sweet “ was inspired by the work of an American artist Wayne Thiebaud, a specialist in painting desserts. I took this inspiration and studied the shapes and silhouettes that are typically seen in cakes and pastries, and translated them into my designs. Pleats and layering were regularly used throughout to mimic the various layers that are often seen in cakes.  Wavy lines resembled the shapes of frosting when squeezed out of a piping bag. Details such as the B/W striped-bias tapes outlining my garments were used to reinterpret Wayne Thiebaud’s use of contrasting colors that outlined his subjects and shadows in his paintings. The colors of the collection were taken directly from one of Wayne Thiebaud paintings, Cakes and Pies. Overall, my intention with this collection was to see fashion in a more lighthearted manner and to find joy in unique designs.
